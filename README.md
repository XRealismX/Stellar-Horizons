# 🌌 Stellar Horizons

> *Rediscover the feeling of a fresh modded experience.*

Stellar Horizons is a tech-exploration modpack built around self-discovery. Modded items and systems are hidden from the start, unlocking silently as you progress through natural milestones — no quest book pointing the way. Expect a gated, multi-tier progression through storage, technology, and beyond, with a hint system for when you're stuck and a world that genuinely pushes back. The endgame goal is building the Dyson Cube. Everything in between is yours to figure out.
